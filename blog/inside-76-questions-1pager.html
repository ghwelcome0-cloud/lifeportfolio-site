우리가 아닌 것 (5)

② 채용·인사 평가 도구가 아님

큰 전환을 겪으면 응답이 자연스럽게 달라짐. → 1~2년 단위 재검사 권유.

The 76 Questions at a Glance — 11 domains × 4 axes × weights

Internal structure of the assessment, disclosed before payment. A manual, not an advertisement.

Four Axes — what we measure

28 items contribute

① Self-Understanding

How deeply you perceive your own thoughts, feelings, beliefs.

12 items contribute

② Self-Expression

How you reveal and communicate your inner state outward.

32 items contribute

③ Self-Design

Your capacity to design directions, criteria, routines for life.

17 items contribute

④ Self-Execution

The drive to turn decisions into action, results, achievement.

89 > 76 because items can contribute to two or three axes simultaneously (N:N mapping).

What we are not (5)

① Not a medical/clinical diagnostic
Different purpose from PHQ-9, GAD-7, etc. → Consult licensed professionals for mental-health concerns.
② Not a hiring/HR tool
No job/salary matching. → Use SHL/Hogan for hiring purposes.
③ Not psychometrically validated yet
Cronbach's α, factor analysis pending. Post-hoc validation planned next quarter.
④ No short-term effect guarantees
We do not promise "your life changes in 3 weeks."
⑤ Not a permanent answer
Responses shift naturally after major life transitions. → Re-take every 1–2 years recommended.
