WHAT · 4 axes of the review

What exactly do we check?

Not a vague "did you live well?" — we measure the exact behaviors you were promised 21 days ago, on 4 axes × 3 questions each. The 4th axis (asset-building) is what makes us different.

Axis A

Mission Alignment

  • How many times did you consciously hold the mission during the 21 days? (0~10)
  • Write your mission in one sentence again (free text)
  • Has the mission become clearer compared to 21 days ago? (-3~+3)
Axis B

3-Action Execution Rate

  • How many times did you execute Action ①? (0~21)
  • How many times did you execute Action ②? (0~21)
  • How many times did you execute Action ③? (0~21)
Axis C

Next 3-Week Clarity

  • Can you picture the next 3 weeks' next move? (0~10)
  • Write the smallest first step for the next 3 weeks (one line)
  • What help would make the next 3 weeks more solid?
Axis D ⭐

Asset-Building Level (our differentiator)

  • Did you leave the 21 days as content/record? (free text + link/file upload)
  • Did anything change in your career, employment, or business?
  • Did any of your 21 days become a pattern for someone else?
