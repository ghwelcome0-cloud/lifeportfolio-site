Q. What am I supposed to do during the 21 days?

Nothing burdensome. Just try, little by little in your daily life, the one-line mission and 3 first actions written in your $14.99 Only One Report. You don't have to do them perfectly.

· A day with 0 attempts and a day with 5 attempts are both meaningful data.
· We won't send reminder emails or set a fixed schedule that nags you. Live at your own pace.
· That's why on day 22 we reach out first: "What scenes did the last 21 days hold for you?"

The 12-question self check-in is just a mirror for looking back honestly. A low score is fine — it's still your real data.
